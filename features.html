Modules

Everything an agency actually runs on

Every module shares the same clients, projects, and people — no re-entering data, no syncing between tools.

◐

Content Workflow & Calendar

A structured pipeline — Brief, Content, Design, Review, Client Approval, Scheduled, Published — with per-stage ownership, time tracking, and direct publishing to Facebook and Instagram.

▢

Client Portal

Each client gets a branded dashboard to approve content, message your team, review deliverables, track leads, browse shared assets, and see their own performance graph.

◆

Pro — AI Assistant

An in-app and WhatsApp-reachable AI assistant that tracks tasks, files, transactions, and can act on recruitment — sending interview invites, offers, and completion reminders.

◈

Meta Insights

Daily-synced Facebook and Instagram analytics — reach, followers, engagement — plus a Best Performing Posts ranking so you know what’s actually working.

◎

Finance & Payroll

Quotes, invoices, subscriptions, and expense tracking — including outstanding/installment payments and per-account-manager commission tracking with automatic monthly or quarterly payout.

◇

Team Career History

Salary raises, promotions, bonuses, commissions, and deductions logged per team member, feeding straight into their prorated monthly payroll — with a one-click email to notify them.

◉

Recruitment & Hiring

A public careers page, AI-scored CV review, structured interview scoring, and digital job offers candidates can accept or negotiate — end to end, without email back-and-forth.

◧

HR & Attendance

Bulk attendance import, leave and work-from-home requests with manager approval, and performance logs — all tied to each team member's own profile.

◫

CRM & Lead Generation

Track leads through a real pipeline from first contact to closed-won, with activity history and AI-assisted lead generation tools.

◰

Assets Library

A shared, folder-organized library for every file — global or scoped to a specific client — so nothing lives only on one person's laptop.

◱

Roles & Permissions

Granular, per-role access control across every module — Clients, Finance, CRM, Assets, HR — so each person only sees what their role should.

FAQ

Common questions

Who is SocialFlow built for?

Social media agencies and in-house marketing teams managing multiple clients — anyone juggling content production, client approvals, finance, and hiring across separate tools.

Do clients need their own login?

Yes — each client gets their own Client Portal account, scoped only to their own content, messages, assets, and performance data.

Does the AI assistant work outside the app?

Yes — Pro is reachable both inside SocialFlow and over WhatsApp, so team members can log a transaction, check a task, or move a recruitment step along without opening the app.

How do I log in?

Use the Log In button at the top of this page — it takes you straight to socialflow.admepro.com, where you sign in with your existing team or client account.
